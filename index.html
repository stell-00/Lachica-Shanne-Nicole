Welcome to the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) Strand

The Senior High School (SHS) Program at Sta. Catalina National High School is the final stage of the K–12 basic education system in the Philippines. It prepares students for college, employment, entrepreneurship, and middle-level skills development. Students choose a specific strand that matches their interests and future career goals.

One of the offered strands is the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) strand. The ICT strand focuses on developing students’ knowledge and practical skills in computer systems, programming, web development, and digital technologies.

By enrolling in the ICT strand at Sta. Catalina National High School, students gain hands-on experience in areas such as coding, computer hardware servicing, multimedia design, and basic networking.
