Program and Activities

The Information and Communication Technology (ICT) strand is designed to equip students with practical knowledge and technical skills related to modern digital technology. The program focuses on preparing students for careers in the information technology industry as well as for further studies in technology-related fields.

Core and Specialized Subjects:

  • Computer Programming
  • Web Development
  • Computer Systems Servicing
  • Multimedia and Digital Design
  • Networking Basics

Skills Developed:

  • Basic programming and coding skills
  • Website design and development
  • Troubleshooting computer hardware and software
  • Digital creativity and multimedia editing
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking in technology

Students also participate in training sessions, industry-based activities, and work immersion programs that allow them to apply their knowledge in real workplace environments. These activities help students gain hands-on experience and build confidence in their technical abilities.
