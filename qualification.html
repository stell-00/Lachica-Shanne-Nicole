Qualification

Students who wish to enroll in the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) strand must first complete their Junior High School education. The ICT strand is ideal for students who are interested in computers, technology, and digital innovation.

Basic Requirements:

  • Completion of Junior High School (Grade 10)
  • School report card and enrollment documents
  • Willingness to learn technical and computer-related skills

Skills and Interests Aligned with the Strand:

  • Interest in computers, software, and digital technology
  • Basic problem-solving and logical thinking skills
  • Creativity in designing digital content or applications
  • Willingness to learn programming and technical skills
  • Ability to work independently and in teams

Students who choose the ICT strand should be motivated to explore modern technology and develop practical skills that are useful in the IT industry. This strand is suitable for learners who want to pursue technology-related courses or careers in the future.
